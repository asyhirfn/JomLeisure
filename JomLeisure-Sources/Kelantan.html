KELANTAN

Kelantan is one of the state in Malaysia.It was located in East Coast Region of Malaysia or called as Peninsular Malaysia.The Capital for Kelantan is Kota Bharu. Kelantan is known as "Land of Lightning" because of the numerous thunderstorms that strike the state every year.Kelantan is a state famous for its green paddy fields, rustic fishing villages and beaches lined with casuarinas. Kelantan culture varies considerably from Malay culture in the rest of the peninsula due to Kelantan's relative isolation and mainly rural lifestyle; this is expressed in the cuisine, arts and the peculiar language of Kelantanese Malay, which is unintelligible even for some standard Malay speakers.When more and more individuals flock to see the rich Malay culture inherent in the lifestyles of the people of Kelantan it's make a tourism as a growing industry.A travel here will definitely immerse you in Malaysia's interpretation of Malay culture and customs.
