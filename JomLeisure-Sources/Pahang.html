PAHANG

Pahang is the third largest state in Malaysia by area and ninth largest by population. The state covers the Pahang River basin, as far south as Endau, and a stretch of the east coast.Geographically situated in the Peninsular Malaysia area on the East Coast, the state shares boundaries with the Kelantan and Terengganu states of Malaysia to the north, Perak, Selangor and Negeri Sembilan to the west, Johor to the south, while the South China Sea is to the east.Almost two-thirds of the 35,000 sq. km of Pahang land area is surrounded by thick tropical rainforests, making it a repository for the natural treasures of Malaysia. In Pahang, you can find almost all of the highland retreats, some beautiful islands and beaches.
