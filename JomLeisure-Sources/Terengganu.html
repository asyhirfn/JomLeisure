TERENGGANU

Terengganu is a a state on the eastern coast of Peninsular Malaysia in Malaysia facing the South China Sea. With a population of just over a million, it is one of nine Sultanates. Terengganu is recognized as a religious, Islamic province, with its nickname Darul Iman(Abode of Faith in Allah). The land area of Terengganu is about 13,305 sq. km. Terengganu is famous among most of tourists that come to Malaysia because of theoir beautiful places. This state is famous with oil & gas, fisheries, agriculture and tourism industries.The primary attractions of the state are the beautiful tropical Perhentian and Redang Islands.
