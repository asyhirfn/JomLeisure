INTRODUCTION

Peninsular Malaysia (Malay: Semenanjung Malaysia), also known as Malaya or West Malaysia, is the part of Malaysia which occupies the southern half of the Malay Peninsula and the surrounding islands. It consists of 11 out of the 13 states, and two out of the three federal territories of Malaysia. The capital city, Kuala Lumpur is also in Peninsular Malaysia that is a very known and most visited place in Malaysia.

West Malaysia is divided into 4 regions:

  • Northern Region: Perlis, Kedah, Penang, Perak
  • East Coast of Malaysia: Kelatan, Terengganu and Pahang.
  • Central Region: Selangor, federal territories of Kuala Lumpur and Putrajaya
  • Southern Region: Negeri Sembilan, Malacca, Johor
