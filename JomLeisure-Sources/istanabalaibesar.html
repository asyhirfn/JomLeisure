ISTANA BALAI BESAR

Istana Balai Besar which is a historic place in Kelantan can be the oldest place in Malaysia. The unique Great Hall is still used for royal ceremonies such as the summit and coronation ceremonies. The palace also serves as an institution to preserve the heritage and tradition of the Malays. Istana Balai Besar is built on 1.6 hectares of land where it was built to replace the old one located on Saba Island on the east coast of Sungai Kelantan. Pulau Saba was the administrative center of the Sultanate in Kelantan before 1842 but due to the erosion caused by the floods which is a matter of almost every year, the center of government has moved to Kota Bharu.
